UW Locations - Your guide to University of Washington campus locations

Getting Started

Welcome to UW Locations! This app helps you find libraries, cafes, buildings, and dining locations across the UW campus.

Key Features

  • Browse Locations: View all UW locations organized by type (Libraries, Cafes, Buildings, Food)
  • Search: Quickly find locations by name or address
  • Filter: Filter by location type or status (Open Now, Closed, Opening Soon)
  • Real-Time Hours: See current hours and status for each location
  • Get Directions: Tap "Get Directions" to open locations in Apple Maps
  • Detailed Information: View contact info, hours, and special dates for each location

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I search for a location?
A: Use the search bar at the top of the app. You can search by location name or address. The search updates as you type.
Q: How do I filter locations?
A: Use the type filter chips below the search bar to filter by Libraries, Cafes, Buildings, or Food. Tap the filter icon in the top right to filter by status (Open Now, Closed, etc.).
Q: How are hours determined?
A: Hours are updated manually by the app developer based on the current academic quarter (Fall, Winter, Spring, Summer). The app also accounts for special dates, breaks, and finals week schedules.
Q: Does the app track my location?
A: No. The app does not request or use your device's location services. It only displays static campus location information.
Q: How do I get directions to a location?
A: Open any location's detail page and tap the "Get Directions" button. This will open Apple Maps with walking directions to the location.
Q: Why don't I see walking times or distances?
A: The app does not use location services, so it cannot calculate distances or walking times. Use the "Get Directions" button to see routes in Apple Maps.
Q: How often is location data updated?
A: Location data and hours are included in the app bundle. Updates are included in app updates from the App Store. Hours automatically adjust based on the current date and academic calendar.
Q: Is this app official from the University of Washington?
A: No. This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the University of Washington. All information is provided for informational purposes only.

Troubleshooting

App won't load locations

  • Make sure you have an internet connection (for initial data load)
  • Try force-closing and reopening the app
  • If the problem persists, try deleting and reinstalling the app

Hours seem incorrect

  • Hours update automatically based on the academic calendar
  • Check if there's a special date or break affecting hours
  • Verify the current quarter (Fall, Winter, Spring, Summer)
  • Some locations may have different hours during breaks or finals week

Search not working

  • Make sure you've typed at least a few characters
  • Try searching by location name or address
  • Check your filters - you may have filtered out all matching locations

App crashes or freezes

  • Force-close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  • Check that your device is running iOS 17.0 or later
